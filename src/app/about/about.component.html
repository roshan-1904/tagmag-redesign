About TAMILNADU MAGNESITE LIMITED

TAMILNADU MAGNESITE LIMITED (TANMAG) is a pioneering public sector undertaking committed to responsible mining and sustainable industrial development. Established with a vision to harness the rich magnesite reserves of Tamil Nadu, we play a crucial role in supplying high-quality raw materials to essential industries, particularly the steel sector. Our operations adhere to strict environmental guidelines, ensuring minimal ecological impact and promoting biodiversity in our mining areas.

With a strong focus: 'Innovation, TANMAG continuously invests in research and development to enhance the quality and versatility of our magnesite products. Our dedicated team of experts works tirelessly to meet the evolving demands of the steel, refractory, and chemical industries, contributing significantly to the nation's industrial self-reliance. We are proud to be a government-owned enterprise, serving the public interest with transparency and accountability.

  • Strategic Importance: A key contributor to national industrial growth and mineral resource management.
  • Sustainable Practices: Committed to environmental stewardship, responsible land reclamation, and community development.
  • Quality & Innovation: Delivering superior magnesite products through continuous process improvement and cutting-edge research.
  • Economic Impact: Generating employment and contributing to the socio-economic development of the region.
